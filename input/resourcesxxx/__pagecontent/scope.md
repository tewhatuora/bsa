# Scope

This Implementation Guide covers the **FHIR R4 data exchange used for CPIR integration within the Breast Screening NZ programme IT solution**.

## In Scope

- Use of **standard FHIR R4 resources**:
    - `Patient`
    - `Appointment`
    - `CarePlan`
    - `EpisodeOfCare`
    - `Organization`
- **Linkage of screening participants** to episodes and appointments
- Representation of **programme enrolment and workflows** using a CarePlan

## Out of Scope

This IG does not cover:

- Complete end-to-end breast screening workflow
- Clinical imaging
- Treatments and post-diagnostic activities
